What is NationStates Census Database?

The NationStates Census Database (NSCDB) is a tool for players of NationStates to get more information about the top 500 players of each census report from the WA (World Assembly). While the original WA census reports for NationStates give player rankings, they do not do a good job of showing the statistics of the types of nations that occupy the top 500 positions on each report.

  • 1.) Shows the percentage of the world of each WA classification (Government Type)
  • 2.) Shows the percentage of each classification of the top 500 nations' economy, political freedom, and civil rights for each WA census
  • 3.) Compare your nation to the reports on this website to make better deicisons to get your desired nation
  • 4.) Get a never before seen picture about the NationStates world at large

Data Points Collected

NationStates does a fairly decent job of displaying data about the "NationStates World". I always found myself asking questions about what the the world actually looked like. There is so much data its really hard to get a good idea of the dominant political systems at play in the world of NationStates. This project started out with a need to know what kinds of nations most people are creating. I used Python and BeatifulSoup to scrape all 83 WA census reports for the top 500 nations of each report for a total of 41,500 nations scraped. Each nation I scraped I got 5 data points: Nation Name, WA Category, Civil Rights, Economy, and Political Freedom. In total, I have 207,500 data points.
